GALLERY PHOTOS — How to add your photos
========================================

Drop your wedding photos into this folder (photos/gallery/) with these exact names:

  photo-1.jpg   → Tall left column  (portrait/vertical photo works best)
  photo-2.jpg   → Top middle
  photo-3.jpg   → Top right
  photo-4.jpg   → Bottom middle
  photo-5.jpg   → Wide bottom row   (landscape/horizontal photo works best)

Tips:
  - JPG or WebP format recommended
  - At least 800px wide for good quality
  - The website will automatically show them once the files are added
  - You can delete this instructions.txt file once you've added your photos
