STORY PHOTO — How to add your couple photo
==========================================

Drop ONE photo into this folder (photos/story/) with this exact name:

  couple.jpg    → Shows in the "Our Story" section on the left side

Tips:
  - Portrait/vertical orientation looks best (taller than wide)
  - JPG or WebP format recommended
  - At least 600px wide for good quality
  - You can delete this instructions.txt file once you've added your photo
