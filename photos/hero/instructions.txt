HERO COVER PHOTO — How to add your photo
=========================================

Drop ONE photo into this folder (photos/hero/) with this exact name:

  cover.jpg    → Shows as the large cover photo on the LEFT side of the main page

Tips:
  - Portrait or landscape both work — the photo fills the full left panel
  - JPG or WebP format recommended
  - At least 1200px tall for good quality on retina screens
  - "object-position: center top" is set by default, so the top of the photo
    is always shown — great for couple portraits where faces are at the top
  - To change the focal point, edit css/style.css and update:
      .hero-photo { object-position: center top; }
    e.g. use "center center" to focus the middle of the photo

What happens when you add the photo:
  - The photo wipes in from the left edge (cinematic reveal animation)
  - Gold sparkles scatter across the photo for ~4 seconds then disappear
  - Your names slide in from the right side simultaneously
  - You can delete this instructions.txt file once you've added your photo
