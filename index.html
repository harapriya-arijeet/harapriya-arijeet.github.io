Our Story

◆ ◆ ◆
♥
Add couple.jpg to
photos/story/

Theirs is a story that began long before the wedding invitations went out. Two lives that crossed paths, two hearts that recognised something in each other — and, without either of them quite planning it, a quiet, honest love that grew stronger with every passing day.

Through long conversations and longer distances, through ordinary days and unspoken doubts, they chose each other again and again. What began as friendship deepened into a partnership — one built on trust, laughter, and the certainty that they wanted to walk the rest of the way together.

And then came the part that took the most courage: bringing their families into the story. With patience, honesty, and no shortage of heartfelt conversations, they slowly turned two families into one. Today, Harapriya and Arijeet stand together not just as two people in love, but as a promise their families chose to bless.

"First we chose each other. Then we brought our families home to that choice."
