BACKGROUND MUSIC — How to add your music
=========================================

Drop your music file into this folder (music/) with this exact name:

  background.mp3   ← required (works in all browsers)
  background.ogg   ← optional fallback for older browsers

WHEN IT PLAYS
  - Music starts automatically after the door opens (gentle fade-in)
  - Volume is set to 30% so it stays soft and doesn't overpower
  - It loops continuously
  - Guests can mute/unmute using the ♪ button in the bottom-right corner

RECOMMENDED MUSIC STYLE
  - Soft shehnai / bansuri / sitar instrumental
  - Classical Indian wedding background music
  - Soft piano or strings
  - Avoid anything with lyrics — it distracts from reading

FILE SIZE
  - Keep under 5 MB for fast loading on mobile
  - Use a 128 kbps MP3 — perfectly fine quality for background music

WHERE TO FIND ROYALTY-FREE MUSIC
  - pixabay.com/music       (search "Indian wedding" or "shehnai")
  - freesound.org
  - mixkit.co/free-music
  - bensound.com

You can delete this instructions.txt once you've added your music file.
